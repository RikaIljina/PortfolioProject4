About this Website

Welcome to MusicLab, a place where artists can share their creations, give and receive constructive feedback, and explore fresh avenues of collaboration.

What will you find here?

We provide a space for musicians of each and every skill level and all genres – from pop ballad and classic rock to experimental House and Klingon opera.

What can you share?

As long as it consists of soundwaves you’ve created with our own mouth, hands, gills, etc. you are welcome to share it. Covers are allowed if tagged accordingly. While not mandatory, other users will also welcome it if you tag NSFW entries and other possible triggering content.

If you want to document a work in progress, check "Keep file" when editing an entry and uploading a new version - the old file will not be deleted.

How to interact with other creators?

We encourage users to like and comment on entries and get in touch with each other via the contact info provided.

When commenting on other people’s works, please remember to be open-minded and constructive. MusicLab reserves the right to delete comments or block users who use abusive and discriminatory language or troll others.

Please also refrain from advertising your own music in the comments section of other creators.

What about ownership and copyright?

MusicLab does not own any of the content hosted on its site. All audio files and the information provided in the description field belong to the creators. If a file is deleted, no copy of any kind remains in the database.

Users are not allowed to re-upload entries other creators have deleted, even if they are tagged as foreign work. MusicLab will block users who do not adhere to this rule.

What about donations and commissions?

MusicLab does not allow users to ask for donations or offer commissions on its website. However, you are welcome to post a link to your own website where such information may be found.
